TREKKER OUTBACK 58

BLACK V56 MAXIA4 TOP CASE WITH BLACK COVER, SMOKED REFLECTORS AND ANODISED ALUMINIUM FINISH

产品参数

Designed for rear placement, this spacious 58-LTR top case attaches to the motorcycle using a MONOKEY® plate. Its full-aluminium square structure with natural finish echoes the typical “Adventure” design of the OUTBACK side cases. The contrasting angular profiles and bumpers in black technopolymer add to the style. Able to comfortably hold 2 modular or full-face helmets, this top case (also available in a 42-LTR version) offers the possibility to attach objects or other bags on top of the lid, thanks to the 4 upper strap loops. The technical features, which also include the Security Lock mechanism and a useful mat for the base that absorbs impact and vibrations, now include optional accessories that allow you to personalise the top case to suit your own taste: from the elasticated net that secures luggage to the underside and the polyurethane backrest, to the internal courtesy light, the waterproof inner bag and many others.
Note: it cannot be fixed to plates equipped with the stop light kit connection.
